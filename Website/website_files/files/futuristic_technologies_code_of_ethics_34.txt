Code of Ethics

Integrity:
Employees are expected to conduct themselves with honesty and integrity in all professional interactions. Avoid conflicts of interest and report any unethical behavior promptly.

Confidentiality:
Protecting confidential information is paramount. Employees must not disclose or misuse sensitive information, including client details, trade secrets, and internal strategies.

Social Responsibility:
Futuristic Technologies is committed to social responsibility. Employees are encouraged to engage in environmentally friendly practices and contribute positively to the community.
